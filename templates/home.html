About Me

Hi! My Name is Emir McGreevy and I am a Junior at the University of Michigan majoring in Statistics and minoring in UX Design

I've been a huge baseball fan my entire life and have always loved watching the Detroit Tigers. Keeping stats at games was one of my favorite activities as a little kid and helped me find my passion for sports statistics. For my SI 339 final project, I wanted to find a fun and creative way to display the statistics for my favorite team. The 2024 season will be talked about by Detroit sports fans for years to come. In August the team had a 0.2% chance at the playoffs, but defied all odds and secured their first playoff spot since 2014.
